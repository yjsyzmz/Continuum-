## Full Description

### English

**You've been there.**

Two hours into a long Claude session: drafting a report, refactoring a project, working through a dense analysis. Then it happens. The 5-hour window runs out. The weekly limit hits. The stream cuts off mid-sentence. Your context, your flow, your half-finished work: gone, or at least painfully hard to recover.

**Continuum makes sure you can always pick up where Claude left off.**

Running quietly in your Claude.ai tab, Continuum captures what's already been generated, tracks your task as it grows, and, the moment things break, hands you a clean, copy-paste-ready prompt to resume in a new chat, a new account, or even a different AI tool.

No re-explaining. No scrolling through dead conversations. No starting over.

**What it does**

- Captures silently: records what Claude streams to your browser, with no extra Claude tokens used.
- Watches your quota: product direction for showing how much runway you have before the wall.
- Generates handoffs: turns any task into a resumable prompt with one click.
- Stays local: everything lives in your browser. No cloud, no Continuum account, no telemetry.

**Who it's for**

Claude Pro users who treat Claude as a workspace, not a chatbot. Writers, developers, researchers, and anyone whose Claude sessions regularly outlast a single quota window.

Open source. No signup. Install and forget until the moment you need it.

### 中文

**这一刻你应该很熟悉。**

你在 Claude 里跑了两个小时：写一份报告、重构一个项目、啃一份复杂材料。然后它来了。5 小时窗口到了。周限额满了。回答在半句话处戛然而止。上下文、节奏、那份做到一半的活：要么没了，要么得花更多时间手动捞回来。

**Continuum 让你永远可以从 Claude 停下的地方继续走。**

它安静地跑在你的 Claude.ai 标签页里，全程记录已经生成的内容，跟踪任务进度，并在事情真的崩掉的那一刻，给你一段可以直接粘贴的接力 prompt。你可以把它粘到新对话、另一个账号，甚至别的 AI 工具里，让任务从断点继续。

不用重新解释，不用翻历史，不用从零开始。

**它做什么**

- 静默捕获：记录 Claude 流式输出的内容，不额外消耗 Claude token。
- 额度感知：产品方向是显示你离 quota 撞墙还有多远。
- 一键接力：任意任务一键生成可粘贴的接力 prompt。
- 完全本地：所有数据都在你浏览器里。不上云、不要 Continuum 账号、不发遥测。

**谁会用它**

把 Claude 当作工作台而非聊天机器人的 Pro 用户。写作者、开发者、研究者，以及任何一次 Claude 会话经常撑爆一个 quota 窗口的人。

开源、免注册。装上就忘了它的存在，直到你真的需要它的那一刻。
